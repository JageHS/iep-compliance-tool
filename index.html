Calculate the percentage of time a student spends with non-disabled peers to determine appropriate placement and ensure compliance with IDEA regulations and MUSER requirements.

MUSER Compliance Guidelines

According to Maine Unified Special Education Regulations (MUSER IV.2.D.&E.):

  • Placement decisions must be based on the student's unique needs
  • Students should be educated with non-disabled peers to the maximum extent appropriate
  • Removal from general education requires justification and documentation
  • Schools must provide a continuum of alternative placements
  • Placement decisions must be reviewed at least annually

Compliance Documentation

MUSER LRE Justification Required

According to IDEA and Maine Unified Special Education Regulations (MUSER), the IEP team must provide justification for any removal from the general education environment. This justification must include:

  • Why the student cannot be educated in general education with supplementary aids and services
  • The alternatives considered and why they were rejected
  • How the student will participate in non-academic activities
  • Documentation of attempted interventions (Tier 1 and Tier 2)

MUSER Documentation Checklist

Ensure these items are documented in the IEP according to MUSER requirements:

  • LRE percentage calculation included in the IEP
  • Placement decision clearly documented (MUSER IV.2.D)
  • Supplementary aids and services specified
  • Parent consent obtained for placement
  • Referral documentation attached (if applicable)
  • Intervention history documented
